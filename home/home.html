Elmer practices at Sydney-Therapy, an independent private clinic providing confidential therapy, counselling & other occupational therapy help on an individual basis, to children, adolescents, couple, married couples & families in Sydney and throughout Australia.

As an occupational therapist, Elmer's service specialisations are in assessment, clinical formulation (or case formulation) & treatment of a range of presenting challenges, including various forms of dementia, mood disorders, anxiety disorders, trauma, various forms of stress, substance use, interpersonal difficulties, social & behavioural issues (such as in children, students, parenting, couples), as well as management of chronic medical illnesses & neurological disorders.
