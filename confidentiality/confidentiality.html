Professional conduct

Client confidentiality applies to occupational therapy, as it does in any other medical practice. When seeking treatment or therapy, only the client/s and therapist know exactly what happens in each session. Clients have the absolute right to confidentially and to be treated with the utmost respect by their therapist, ensuring trust and dependency.

We take the privacy of our clients and families very seriously

  • We won't use your personal data for any purpose that you have not agreed to.
  • We won't discuss your child or give your personal data to any company, organisation or individual outside of Elmers Occupational Therapy unless you've provided us with your written consent.
  • We will not sell or trade your personal data to third party list brokers or direct marketing companies.
  • We'll do our very best to ensure that your personal data is kept safe and secure for as long as your details are in our care.
  • Once our relationship has been concluded, we guarantee that your confidential information will be destroyed at an appropriate time in the future
